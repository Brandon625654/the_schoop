In Focus

A closer look at one story shaping the continent

Djibouti and Ethiopia recommit to a shared trade corridor

For more than 95% of Ethiopia's external trade, the road runs through Djibouti. This month, officials from both countries reaffirmed their commitment to improving efficiency on the corridor — addressing customs harmonisation, infrastructure investment, and digital coordination.

The renewed partnership is expected to reduce dwell times, lower trader costs, and demonstrate what Horn-of-Africa economic integration can look like in practice.
